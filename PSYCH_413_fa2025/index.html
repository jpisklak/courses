Why this site exists (a.k.a. Why we're not using Canvas)

  • Clutter-free: There are aren't multiple panels and sidebars fighting for your attention.
  • Speed matters: Pages load in seconds, not semesters.
  • Fewer mystery clicks: No "Modules → Week → Sub-page → External Tool → Where am I?😵‍💫" chain.
  • Searchable: You can actually locate the file you wanted on purpose.
  • Stable: No “please sign in again” right after you signed in again.
  • Readable: Notifications you can read, not decipher (see this font: it's literally the best).
