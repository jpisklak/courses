Purpose of the Class Discord Server

The class Discord server is designed to enhance communication and foster collaboration throughout the term. Here's what you can expect:

  1. Instructor Q&A Forum
    The server includes a dedicated, Reddit-esque forum where you can publicly ask the instructor questions about course material, assignments, and exams. This ensures everyone can benefit from shared answers and discussions.
  2. Student Collaboration
    A variety of text and voice channels are available for you to connect with your classmates. Use these spaces to discuss lecture topics, work on group projects, share study tips, or form study groups.

Accessing the Class Discord Server

To join the class Discord server, you'll need an invitation link. Invitation links are sent to your @ualberta.ca email address at the start of the term. Be sure to check your inbox for the email.

If the invitation link has expired, or you enrolled in the course after the initial email was sent, please contact the instructor or teaching assistant to request a new invitation link.

We look forward to seeing you on Discord!
