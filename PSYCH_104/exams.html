General Exam Information

The following applies to all exams in this course:
  1. Closed book
  2. Format: Multiple choice.
  3. Required Materials: Pencil and good eraser. A calculator is permitted for the second midterm and final exam only.
  4. Content: Includes all lecture content and any assigned readings (noted in class) up to the exam date, unless explicitly stated otherwise. Only the final exam is cumulative, the two midterms are not.
  5. Students with approved academic accommodations will write exams with the Academic Success Centre's Proctoring Services (not in the lecture hall).
  6. Exam Viewing Information
  7. Missed Midterm Exam Session Information
  8. Sample Exam

Calculators

  • Permitted Calculators: Only basic or scientific calculators are allowed for the second midterm and final.
  • Prohibited Calculators: Graphing calculators, programmable calculators, or calculators on phones/devices are NOT allowed in any of the exams.
  • Bring Your Own: Students must bring an approved calculator; none will be provided. Without one, you'll complete the exam without it.
  • Academic Integrity: Unauthorized calculators will result in academic misconduct reports.
  • Unsure about your calculator? Check the approved list or ask the instructor before the exam.
