Purpose of the Class Discord Server

The class Discord server is designed to enhance communication and foster collaboration throughout the term. Here's what you can expect:

  1. Class Forum
    The server includes a dedicated, Reddit-esque forum where you can publicly ask the instructor and your fellow students questions about course material, assignments, and exams. This ensures everyone can benefit from shared answers and discussions.
  2. Student Collaboration
    A variety of text and voice channels are available for you to connect with your classmates. Use these spaces to discuss lecture topics, work on group projects, share study tips, or form study groups.

Accessing the Class Discord Server

To join the class Discord server, you'll need an invitation link. This will be provided during the first lecture.

If the invitation link has expired, please contact the instructor or teaching assistant to request a new invitation link.

We look forward to seeing you on Discord!
