🌐 Welcome to our Course Website 🌐

You might be wondering, "Why does this page look like it was coded on a Windows 95 machine?" It's because we are going to use Canvas as little as possible.

The Deal with Canvas:

After using Canvas for three courses last semester, I can confidently state that (on the instructor-side of things) it was a nice exercise in frustration. So, I've gone rogue and created something much better: a simple, fast, and refreshingly basic HTML page with no pointless notifications and no cumbersome fluff. Think of it as a love letter to the golden era of the internet, when websites were fun, snappy, straightforward, and (most importantly) interesting. 🌟

What This Means for You:

  • 🚀 Faster Loading: No waiting for Canvas to think about whether it feels like opening your page today.
  • 🧼 Zero Clutter: Just the info you need—clean, organized, and easy to find!
  • 🎨 Customizable Coolness: We could (in theory) add a touch of fun while keeping everything straightforward. Maybe there will be easter eggs?
  • 🕹️Retro vibes, modern convenience: You’re getting a taste of the 90s internet with none of the dial-up agony.
  • 📁No hidden corners: Everything is laid out like a well-organized desktop (yes, that was a thing once).
  • 🔒 No Sneaky Data Snatching: This site isn’t spying on you, tracking your clicks, or asking for your life story. It's here to teach, not sell your data to the highest bidder.
  • 🔕 No Pointless Alerts: This site won’t flood your inbox with "important updates" about things you don't need to care about. It respects your peace and quiet!

Pro Tip: Bookmark this page (Ctrl+D or Command+D) for easy access.
